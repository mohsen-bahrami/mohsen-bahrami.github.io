About me

I'm a Research Scientist at MIT Connection Science Group in MIT Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS).

My unwavering passion lies in the exciting realm of computational social science, where I thrive on harnessing the power of data-driven behavioral analytics.

In my fascinating journey, I unravel the intricate patterns hidden within vast and diverse datasets from multiple industries.

I revel in the art of deciphering, analyzing, and predicting both individual and group behaviors.

From this wealth of information, I strive to extract insights that can empower businesses and authorities to make well-informed evidence-backed decisions.

Beyond just data analysis, my ultimate drive is to make a meaningful impact on communities lifestyles.

I firmly believe that data, when skillfully harnessed, holds the potential to elevate community awareness and shape a brighter and more informed tomorrow.
